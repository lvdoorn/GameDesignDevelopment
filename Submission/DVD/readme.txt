Description:
The game Survival of the Zargs is a 2D collaborative story-driven game in 
which players experience an emergency landing with their spaceship on a 
foreign planet. They try to escape the planet again by working together as a 
team, while the game forces them to make difficult decisions all the time. 
Coordination outside of the game is the key to success.
